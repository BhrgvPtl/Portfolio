Customer Churn Early-Warning System

Activated a predictive churn signal for a subscription SaaS platform, integrating model outputs into marketing automation to drive timely, personalized outreach.

Role

Lead Data Scientist

Timeline

16 weeks

Impact

11% churn reduction • +$3.2M ARR retained

Business Problem

Renewals for mid-market customers were lagging, and marketing teams lacked visibility into which accounts were at the highest risk of churn. Without a reliable early warning system, customer success managers were triaging based on anecdotal signals and losing renewal conversations to competitors.

Approach

  1. Partnered with marketing ops to define success metrics and deployment requirements.
  2. Ingested product telemetry, billing, and support interactions via dbt into Snowflake.
  3. Engineered recency-based health signals and customer engagement aggregates.
  4. Trained LightGBM models with monotonic constraints to retain business interpretability.
  5. Deployed scoring jobs with Airflow and exposed features through a feature store API.

Tools & Methods

  • dbt + Snowflake
  • Airflow
  • Python & LightGBM
  • MLflow
  • Fivetran
  • HubSpot

Data Story

Exploratory analysis revealed that product adoption stalled roughly 60 days before renewal for accounts that eventually churned. Support ticket spikes and payment delays were the next leading signals. We crafted a narrative showing executives how combining these behaviors creates an actionable lead score. Weekly insights decks highlighted health trends across cohorts, enabling proactive outreach campaigns.

Results

  • Retained $3.2M in annual recurring revenue within the first two quarters post-launch.
  • Cut outreach prioritization time from 6 hours to 35 minutes per CSM weekly sync.
  • Achieved 0.78 precision at 20% recall, exceeding finance team’s ROI threshold.
  • Implemented automated drift detection alerts integrated with Slack and PagerDuty.

Visual Storytelling

Productionization

The scoring pipeline runs nightly using Airflow with CI/CD enforced through GitHub Actions. dbt tests ensure freshness and referential integrity before model inference executes. Model artifacts are tracked in MLflow, and the marketing automation triggers are gated behind feature flagging to support experimentation and rollback.

Explore the Code

Browse the GitHub repository for modular dbt models, feature store configuration, and the MLOps deployment scripts. Each directory contains README files explaining the design choices and reproducibility steps.

Next Steps

We are currently piloting reinforcement learning to optimize intervention timing and adding multi-touch attribution to better quantify campaign influence on retention.
